FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization, donations, and volunteer opportunities.

Are my donations tax-deductible?
Yes, we are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records immediately after donating.
How are funds allocated?
91% of every dollar goes directly to our programs. 8% covers administrative costs and 1% supports fundraising efforts. We publish detailed financial reports annually.
How can I volunteer?
You can apply to volunteer through our Get Involved page. We have opportunities both locally and internationally, with flexible time commitments ranging from a few hours to ongoing roles.
Can I set up recurring donations?
Absolutely! Monthly donations provide us with sustainable funding. You can choose any amount and cancel anytime through your donor dashboard. Recurring donations are especially impactful for long-term programs.
Do you work internationally?
Yes, we currently operate in 12 countries across 4 continents, partnering with local organizations to deliver sustainable programs that respect local cultures and needs.
