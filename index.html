Our Mission

Building a Better World Together

At Captain Foundation AI, we are driven by the belief that lasting change starts at the community level. Through grassroots partnerships and sustainable programs, we empower local leaders to create solutions that endure long after our direct involvement.

Education

Building schools, training teachers, and providing scholarships to unlock potential in every child.

Healthcare

Delivering mobile clinics, clean water, and preventive care to communities with limited access.

Sustainability

Promoting clean energy, reforestation, and sustainable farming for long-term community resilience.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my donations tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations to Captain Foundation AI are tax-deductible. You will receive an official receipt via email immediately after your donation is processed.
How are funds allocated?
92% of every dollar goes directly to our programs. 6% covers administrative costs and 2% supports fundraising efforts to expand our reach.
How can I volunteer?
You can apply to volunteer through our Get Involved page. We have opportunities both locally and internationally, with flexible time commitments.
Can I set up recurring donations?
Absolutely! Monthly donations provide us with sustainable funding. You can choose any amount and cancel anytime through your donor dashboard.
Do you work internationally?
Yes, we currently operate in 15 countries across 4 continents, partnering with local organizations to deliver sustainable programs.
