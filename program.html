Clean Water Initiative in Sub-Saharan Africa

Our flagship clean water program is transforming lives across rural communities in Sub-Saharan Africa. By combining sustainable infrastructure with AI-powered monitoring, we ensure that clean water access lasts for generations.

Since launch, we've built 340+ wells serving over 200,000 people, with 98% still operational after 5 years.
